# v0: How did we get here?

This was a random thought that struck me at around 7 PM on May 27th, 2026. "WHAT is AI/ML?". Until then I was perfectly content doing "backend development". Guess I've a new favourite now.

Trying to understand things from first principles was fun. It helped built a lot of inuition for the things I've learnt later on. I'd say after figuring out the limits of a FeedForward Network and understanding the need for RNNs and LSTMs and the hundred other architectures and optimizations that have been introduced in the past few decades, you start hitting the flat tail of learning via intuition.

`v0` is very primitive, it only uses a single neuron to decompose `linear` logic gates like AND and OR.
